About Me

Hey there! I'm thrilled to share my story as I transition back to the workforce as a Frontend Engineer, following a break to care for my newborn. My professional path has been diverse with valuable experiences.

In my recent role as a Senior Frontend Engineer at Stacklet, I was the first on board, spearheading the development of Stacklet's UI with React, Relay, GraphQL, and MaterialUI. I managed authentication, built an internal component library, and collaborated closely with designers and product managers for a user-centric experience.

Before Stacklet, I served as a Software Engineer at Fountain, focusing on SPA development, performance optimization, and mobile responsiveness. I also took on contract roles as a Frontend Engineer, building admin applications and implementing user authentication for Sqwad.

My journey started at Google as a Bilingual Engineer, improving search results. Additionally, I have a strong foundation in Mechanical Design Engineering from my time at Konan Sekkei, Japan.

My academic background includes a Master of Science in Engineering Management from the University of Southern California. I earned my Bachelor's in Automotive Design and Manufacturing Engineering from Chulalongkorn University in Thailand.

I'm passionate about innovation, problem-solving, and building impactful user experiences. My unique blend of experiences and skills is ready to make a positive contribution to your team and projects!
